About me

I am a final-year PhD candidate at Stony Brook University, advised by Prof. Fan Ye.

My research focuses on building smart and connected systems for passive and continuous in-home health monitoring (computational screening and surveillance, smart aging). My investigation spans across multiple disciplines, including ubiquitous computing, wireless sensing, Internet of Things, cyber-physical-human systems, and machine learning.

I am on the academic job market this year. Please reach out for any opportunities!
